# AutoBrowse — Requirements as User Stories

All stories below are implemented in the prototype.

## Vehicle Library & Admin
- As a shopper, I want to **log a new vehicle** I'm considering (make, model, year, trim, body style, condition, mileage, color, dealer, listing URL), so my whole shortlist lives in one place.
- As a shopper, I want to **edit** any vehicle's details, so I can keep information current as I learn more.
- As a shopper, I want to **duplicate** a vehicle (auto-tagged "scenario"), so I can model alternate pricing or financing on the same car without re-entering everything.
- As a shopper, I want to **delete** a vehicle I added by mistake.
- As a shopper, I want to **exclude (set aside)** a vehicle I've ruled out **and record why** (over budget, too small, didn't like the drive, reliability, a better option won, no longer available, or my own note), so I remember my reasoning later.
- As a shopper, I want an excluded car to **automatically drop out of comparisons, rankings, and the matrix**, so my analysis only reflects cars I'm actually considering.
- As a shopper, I want to **restore** an excluded car to my shortlist in one click, so setting aside is never destructive.
- As a shopper, I want to **filter** my garage by "in consideration" vs "excluded" and by powertrain, and **sort** by price, payment, rating, economy, or name, so I can find cars quickly.

## Vehicle Details & Specs
- As a shopper, I want to **store full specs** (engine, horsepower, torque, transmission, drivetrain, economy/MPGe, EV range, battery, seating, cargo, towing, dimensions, legroom, ground clearance), so I can compare on the numbers that matter.
- As a shopper, I want the app to **show EV-specific fields for EVs** and fuel-economy for gas/hybrids, so I'm not entering irrelevant data.
- As a shopper, I want a **free-text notes** field per vehicle for my running thoughts.

## Personal Ratings & Test Drive
- As a shopper, I want to **score each car 1–10** across comfort, driving enjoyment, interior quality, technology, appearance, cargo, and value, and see an **overall average**, so my subjective impressions are captured consistently.
- As a shopper, I want to **record test-drive impressions** (ride quality, visibility, seat comfort, cabin quietness, acceleration, steering feel) as both a score and a note, so I don't forget how each car felt.

## Pricing, Finance, Lease & Ownership
- As a shopper, I want to track **pricing** (MSRP, selling price, discounts, incentives, trade-in, tax rate, fees) and see a live **out-the-door** total.
- As a shopper, I want to model a **finance scenario** (down payment, APR, term) and instantly see monthly payment, amount financed, total interest, and total loan cost.
- As a shopper, I want to model a **lease scenario** (term, residual %, drive-off, mileage, money factor) and see monthly lease, total lease cost, and buyout.
- As a shopper, I want to estimate **cost of ownership** (annual mileage, fuel/electricity price, insurance, maintenance) projected over **1 / 3 / 5 years**, so I can compare true running costs — including EV energy vs gas.

## Side-by-Side Comparison (the centerpiece)
- As a shopper, I want to **compare vehicles side-by-side** across specs, ratings, test-drive scores, pricing, finance, lease, and ownership, so I can weigh them on one screen.
- As a shopper, I want the **best value in each row highlighted automatically**, so winners jump out without manual scanning.
- As a shopper, I want to **toggle which categories** appear and **add/remove vehicles**, so I can focus the comparison.
- As a shopper, I want to **rank the columns** by price, payment, lease, 5-year cost, economy, rating, or power.

## Decision Matrix & Ranking
- As a shopper, I want to **assign weights** to the factors I care about (price, comfort, seat comfort, rear legroom, ownership cost, interior, etc.), so the app reflects *my* priorities.
- As a shopper, I want every car scored **0–100 and ranked** with a visible **contribution breakdown** per factor, so I can see *why* a car ranks where it does — the spreadsheet replacement.

## Dashboard & Attachments
- As a shopper, I want a **dashboard** showing my top match, best monthly payment, lowest 5-year cost, and recently viewed cars, so I get an at-a-glance read on my shortlist.
- As a shopper, I want to **attach** window stickers, quotes, photos, and PDFs to a vehicle, so supporting material stays with the car.

## Persistence & Personalization
- As a shopper, I want my data **saved automatically on my device** with no account, so I can close and return anytime.
- As a shopper, I want to **personalize each car's accent color** and the app's theme/density, so the workbook feels like mine.
